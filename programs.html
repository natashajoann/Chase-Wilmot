Group Coaching

CURRENT PROGRAM

To Be Heard: A Course on Deep Listening | 5-WEEK PROGRAM | September-October 2020

I’m finding that in these unique times we are living in, one of the most important skills we can practice is Listening. It’s an avenue of deep solidarity and support to whoever you are listening to, and on the flip side of that, the experience of being heard, fully and truly, can be healing on so many levels. 

Additionally, the program will explore the topic of intuition and listening to our inner guidance and wisdom as a key tool to being in a healthy purposeful relationship to oneself.

Deep Listening has the potential to cultivate huge growth and connection for everyone who partakes on all sides of the experience.

This program will be an exploration of:

  • How to make someone feel deeply heard

  • Looking for the answers within

  • Intuition

  • Uncovering what people are *really* saying

  • Discovering what YOU are *really* saying

  • The ‘spiritual’ context

This course is for YOU if:

  • You are looking for ways to heal important relationships in your life

  • You are aching for some extra emotional support

  • You are looking for a loving and supportive community

  • You struggle with overwhelm and anxiety around the changes of our world

  • You are interested in coming into greater awareness of your spiritual self and developing tools to help you do so

  • You want to learn how to better ‘hold-space’ for people at work or in your personal relationships

  • You want to deepen your relationship with yourself

Each call will be structured in a way that each participant will get an opportunity to listen and feel heard. The program will be a container of great support and love for you as you develop practical skills and as you look to places that could use extra healing and support.

I will be leading short group meditations with short journal prompts to follow, to give time to cultivate the skill of listening to one’s Inner Guidance. There will be opportunities for break out discussions and facilitation for each participant to learn to hold space for others in the group as well as develop connections with fellow participants. As well as ample time and space for coaching and support from me!

Details:

The sessions will be on Monday nights from 5-6:30PM starting on Sept. 28th - Oct. 26th, via ZOOM.

The fee is $333.
